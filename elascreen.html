Elascreen

ElaScreen uses a elastic screen to give user a tangible experience with natural force feedback. It quite works similar to a 2D touchpad but enables 3-dimensional input by pressing and pulling action on the screen. It can be utilized for navigation of 3-dimensional information such as CAD models, and also for implementing force feedback on information visualization.
