ElaScreen

ElaScreen uses a elastic screen to give user a tangible experience with natural force feedback. It quite works similar to a 2D touchpad but enables 3-dimensional input by pressing and pulling action on the screen.

It can be utilized for navigation of 3-dimensional information such as CAD models, and also for implementing passive force feedback on information visualization.

SIGCHI2013, Extended Abstracts, by Kyungwon Youn, Junbong Song, Keehong Youn, Sungmin Cho, Hyunwoo Bang
