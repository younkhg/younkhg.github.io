Balanced Movement

  • Realtime audiovisual, Custom C++ software
  • Curved screen projection with 2 projectors
  • 3360 × 1080 resolution video, 2 Channel audio
  • Video link (vimeo.com)

This is a new iteration of the Balanced Movement with a fixed length composition. Visual style was changed and the software was modified to show the work on a curved projection screen. Balanced Movement was exhibited at the Media Arts and Technology End of Year Show 2019, UC Santa Barbara.
