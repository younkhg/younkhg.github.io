Spherical Flocking

Based on flocking rule of Craig W. Reynolds a modified rule was designed and applied to agents to simulate behavior of group of fishes underwater. Fishes are presented in shadow, for dark silhouette to give a feeling of deep see where lights only comes from above, with water ripples shape.

The scene gets rendered in full surrounding spherical viewport so that it can be projected into the inner surface of 5-meter-radius sphere, the AlloSphere at UCSB. Positions of fishes will also spatialize the sound inside the sphere, with help of the full 3-D spatial audio setup of the AlloSphere. All visual and sound are generated in realtime.
