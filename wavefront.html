Wavefront

Full immersive projection space

Realtime audiovisual, Custom C++ software

Wavefront is a full immersive audiovisual presented in AlloSphere. Three dimensinoal wavelets are designed with the inspiration from ocean waves. Then a large number of them are spawned in space, creating a complex scenary with the full surround visual and spatialized sound.
